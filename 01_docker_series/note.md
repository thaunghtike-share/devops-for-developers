# 📅 Day 1: Architecture & Isolation Logic

### 🧠 Learning Objectives
- Understand the problem Docker solves (Environment Drift)
- Learn Container vs. VM architecture
- Understand the "Pillars of Isolation"

### 📚 Modules
- **The Problem:** "It works on my machine" and dependency hell.
- **The Technology:** Containerization vs. Virtualization (Kernel Sharing vs. Hypervisor).
- **Docker Architecture:** The Client-Server model (CLI vs. Daemon).
- **Isolation Pillars:** Deep dive into Namespaces (View) and Cgroups (Limits).
- **Mental Model:** Image (Blueprint) vs. Container (Instance).
